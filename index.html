Lasagna Sausage Recipe

Lasagna with sausage is a delicious and hearty Italian dish that typically consists of layers of pasta, tomato sauce, cheese, and sausage. The sausage can be either sweet or spicy, depending on your preference, and is often crumbled and mixed into the tomato sauce.

The pasta used in lasagna sausage is typically long, wide strips of lasagna noodles, which are boiled until al dente and then layered with the tomato sauce and sausage mixture. The tomato sauce is usually made with crushed tomatoes, garlic, onions, and a variety of herbs and spices, such as oregano and basil, to give it a rich, savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (around 12-16 pieces, depending on the size of your baking dish)
  • 1 pound of sweet or spicy Italian sausage (casings removed)
  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) of tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse the noodles with cold water to prevent them from sticking together.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the sausage over medium heat until it is no longer pink, breaking it up into small pieces with a spoon or spatula
  4. Add the garlic and onion to the skillet and cook until they are softened and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a small mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix well.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping each other.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture on top of the noodles, followed by a layer of the sausage and tomato sauce mixture
  10. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce
  11. Repeat steps 8-10 until you have used up all of the ingredients, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the noodles are fully cooked
  14. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil, if desired.

Tuna Fish Salad Recipe

Tuna fish salad is a classic and delicious dish that is easy to prepare and perfect for a healthy and satisfying meal. The recipe typically consists of canned tuna mixed with mayonnaise and other ingredients to create a flavorful and nutritious salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cans of Tuna Fish, drained
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
